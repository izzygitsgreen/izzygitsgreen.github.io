The morning after I arrived in Fargo, I toured five apartments in the Fargo/West Fargo area. Here was my criteria when searching for apartments.

  • Within walking distance of restaurants and shops
  • New construction
  • Studio (separate bedroom area) or 1 bedroom
  • Washer/dryer in unit
  • Indoor parking
  • Hardwood floors

Most of the apartments I toured were newly constructed with washer/dryers in the units and walking distance from stores and restaurants. However, many had concrete flooring instead of hardwood flooring, were further from downtown than I would have liked, or were near areas that did not feel as safe.


Contenders

Ultimately, I decided on Apartment 3! It had all of the features I listed in my apartment criteria, as well as the location in the middle of downtown. Right outside the lobby, there are coffee shops, fitness studios, restaurants, and boutiques.
