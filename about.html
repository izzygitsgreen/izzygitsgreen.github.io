My Story

Hi! I'm Isabella, and I am a recent college graduate just starting out in a job in tech. During my years in school, I interned in investment management and marketing, and completed additional coursework in technology. This collection of experiences helped me get to where I am today working in tech, and opened up some creative doors along the way. I really enjoy web design, creating and absorbing online content, and am currently working on improving my mediocre photography skills. I love seeing creativity at work in an entrepreneurial setting, and hope to get more involved with business-minded creatives in the future.

Trajectory

Some of my stops along the way...

North Jersey I grew up here spending long days at the region's many lakes, and scouting out nearby NYC.

Boston, MA Fast forward some years, and I moved up to the Boston area for school where I studied international relations and interned in downtown Boston. I recently graduated.

Chicago, IL I interned here last summer, and learned that Chicago is the most magical city in the summertime. The restaurants have outdoor eating spaces, rooftop bars line the river, and the city puts on festivals all summer!

Miami Beach, FL I have spent a lot of time here over the years visiting family. I recently returned home from a trip, and have been looking for Cuban cafes ever since. No city does mamey shakes like Miami Beach.

San Francisco, CA Tech + hiking + city + seeing my SO! What's not to like?

Fargo,ND Just moved here! It is quaint for sure. I will update as I learn more!
