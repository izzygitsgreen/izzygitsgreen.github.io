KFresh Market Website

(a client project)

The Kinnelon Farmer's Market, "KFresh Market", will be launching in June 2021, and I worked with them through the ideation, research, wireframing, prototyping, and interation phases to launch the site.

Final Product

Problem: The KFresh Market needed a website where attendees could locate information about the market, vendors could register to sell goods, and volunteers could sign up to help with organizing and running the market.

The Project: Create and launch a website for the KFresh Market that allows potential guests to learn more so they can attend, and that enables vendors and volunteers to register.

Methods: Brainstorming, Research, Wireframing, Prototyping, Visual Design, Iteration

Tools: Sketching, Miro, Figma, Adobe Illustrator, Unsplash, Network Solutions CMS

Duration: 2 months

Type: Client Project

Iteration



Conducted some user tests and had users go through the prototypes. Using the feedback I gathered, I made some changes to the final product.


Changes Made

  1. Removed the top three buttons - they were redundant as users pointed out they were able to do most if not all of the functionality required from the bottom buttons
  2. Users pointed out that there wasn't a clear way to "like" the slides, since clicking was reserved for the reservation step. I added in a "like" feature when users click up so that the slides save in their profile.
  3. Changed the black outline to a blue/green outline because users commented that the black border made the interface look smaller.

Final Product

Retrospective:


Changes I would make in the future to get more accurate results.

  • Increase the sample size of users surveyed and amount of users who tested the prototype
  • Use video content instead of slides to showcase a place better and capture user's interest in going to a place
  • Expand upon the price and date options further, and look into different mechanisms of showing users all of their options when booking an experience or trip
